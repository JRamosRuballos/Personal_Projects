# Blackjack

This is an implementation of the casino game Blackjack. This follows the regular
rules of the game, where the goal is to get a hand value as close to 21 without
exceeding it. The available cards are made up of multiple decks, in this case,
it would be infinite decks. Therefore, the probability of drawing a specific
card remains the same for each card drawn.

Point System:
- Cards 2-10 are worth their face value
- Face Cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10
- Aces can be 1 or 11

Standard Deck of Cards:
- The suits are hearts, diamonds, spades, and clubs
- Each suit contains 13 cards: Ace, 2-10, Jack, Queen, and King
- A total of 52 different playing cards
- hearts and diamonds are red suits
- clubs and spades are black suits
